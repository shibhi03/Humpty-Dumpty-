FAQ

What are the class schedules?

Our classes are held from Monday to Saturday, with the following timings:
- Morning Session: 6:00 AM - 1:30 PM
- Evening Session: 3:30 PM - 9:00 PM

What are the fees for the courses?

The course fees vary depending on the duration and level of the course. Please contact us for detailed information about the fees.

What is your teaching methodology?

We follow a communicative approach to language teaching, focusing on practical usage and interactive learning. Our classes include a combination of speaking, listening, reading, and writing activities to enhance language skills.

Can I join the classes mid-way?

Yes, you can join the classes at any time. We offer flexible enrollment options to accommodate new students throughout the course duration.

Is there any age restrictions?

Our courses are open to learners of all ages. We have programs designed for children, teenagers, and adults to cater to different age groups and language learning needs.
